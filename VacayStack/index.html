06Honest answers

Questions
you should ask.

Why free? What's the actual catch?

The site needs real properties to be real. A portfolio of three handmade owner sites is worth more to us than any monthly fee right now — it's how we'll convince our next thirty customers we know what we're doing.

What if I love it but don't want to use VacayStack later?

You keep the site. The code is yours, the copy is yours, the domain is yours. We'd love to keep working with you — but there's no contractual hold. Walking away costs you nothing.

Will it sync with Airbnb / VRBO / Booking?

Not yet. Phase 1 is the scroll-telling website plus a dedicated CRM to manage it. Phase 2 — Comming soon, shipping later this year — is the full PMS: channel sync, multi-property management, and direct payments, all from the same tool. Design partners go first.

What kind of properties are you looking for?

Owner-operated short-term rentals in the US — cabins, beach houses, design-forward homes, single boutique units. The aesthetic of the site rewards a property with a strong sense of place. One to ten properties is the sweet spot.

What if my photos aren't great?

Honest answer — they don't need to be magazine-grade, but the better they are, the better the site looks. We'll guide you on a one-hour reshoot with your phone if needed. We've seen iPhone 14 photos make a beautiful site.

How many design partners are you taking?

Three. We picked the number small on purpose — small enough that we can spend real time on each one, large enough that we learn what generalizes. After three, we'll have a paid offering.
